Strategy Mitigate Mitigate Retain Transfer Avoid
Plan To prevent miscommunication in the high school library renovation project it is important to establish clear communication protocols, conduct regular meetings, utilize project management tools, and to encourage open communication with stakeholders. Working together to lift heavy items or move equipment. Provide safety trainnning. Ensuring all employees have PPE and knows how to operate the equipment. Safety and function checks on equipment prior to use. To accept this risk, there should be a pre-construction plan, that involves confirming material orders and delivery schedules while also accounting for potential weather delays. To transfer this risk, the mechanical and electrical (M/E) system failures should be handled by another entity, typically third-party service provider or contractor with expertise in such systems. This helps shift the responsibility for managing and resolving these risks to an external body. To avoid the risk of damage during furniture installation and other related activities, protective measures can be put in place. By implementing protective coverings on walls before moving furniture, the risk of scratches or damage is prevented entirely.
